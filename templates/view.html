Instructions

To define a "catch-all" alias that handles any address not otherwise specified, just use @{{.Domain}} as the alias.

Destinations are comma-separated email addresses. Use "nobody" if you want the email to be silently dropped.

To block an email address that is receiving spam, use "spam", "SPAM" or a more specific message like "550 I never signed up for your stupid mailing list".
